Panchakarma Treatment
Panchakarma, a cornerstone of Ayurvedic therapy, detoxifies the body and restores balance. Our Panchakarma treatments include five procedures Vamana, Virechana, Basti, Nasya, and Raktamokshana.
Ayurvedic Massage Therapy
Experience the therapeutic benefits of Ayurvedic massage at Ceylon Hospitals. Our skilled therapists use traditional techniques and herbal oils to provide deep relaxation, improve circulation, and promote healing.
Herbal Medicine
Our Ayurvedic practitioners prescribe personalized herbal formulations to treat various ailments and enhance overall health. These natural remedies work in harmony with your body to support maintain long-term wellness.
Yoga and Meditation
We offer guided sessions combining physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation techniques to improve flexibility, reduce stress, and enhance mental clarity.
Ayurvedic Beauty Treatments
Ceylon Hospitals offers a variety of natural beauty therapies, including facials, body scrubs, and hair treatments, using herbal ingredients and traditional techniques.
Stress Management Programs
Our stress management programs combine Ayurvedic therapies, yoga, meditation, and personalized lifestyle counseling to help you cope with stress effectively.
